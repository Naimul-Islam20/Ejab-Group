If you are willing to make a career with a growth oriented organization, where you will be able to use your analytical skills and aptitude to excel, then MGI is the right place for you.

Talent Management & Skill Development

At MGI, we believe in nurturing our talent through comprehensive management and skill development programs. Our focus is on unlocking the potential of every employee by providing tailored learning opportunities, leadership training, and continuous professional growth. We are committed to helping you build the skills needed to excel in your role today and prepare for the challenges of tomorrow. Join us to grow, lead, and thrive in your career at MGI.

Employee Engagement

At MGI, we intend to uplift employee performance by allowing employees to participate in various outdoor team building activities. MGI has integrated recreational outdoor activities for its employees to create a sense of belongingness towards co-workers. These outdoor engagements have been successful in creating positivity, cross-departmental understanding and increase in productivity and boosting creativity.

Diversity & Inclusive Culture

At MGI, we believe that an inclusive culture is essential for long-term success. We celebrate diversity and ensure that every employee feels valued and respected. Our leaders are committed to fostering an environment where all voices are heard and everyone has equal opportunities to thrive. By actively participating in employee engagement activities, they help cultivate a positive and energetic workplace. As we continue to grow, our dedication to maintaining a vibrant and dynamic organizational culture remains unwavering.

Event & Activation

Our vibrant events at MGI, from in-house activities to external seminars, conferences, job fairs, and factory programs, reflect our commitment to creating opportunities for innovation and career development.

A Place for future Leader

MGI intends to harness the potential of the people. We see our people as a leader for next generation who will create an impact on lives.

EMPLOYER BRANDING

We pride ourselves on being more than just an employer—we are a community where every individual is valued and empowered. Our commitment to fostering a supportive, innovative, and inclusive environment sets us apart in the industry. We believe that when our employees thrive, our organization thrives, and that's why we invest in your growth, well-being, and success. By joining MGI, you become part of a forward-thinking team dedicated to making a meaningful impact. Discover a workplace where your potential is realized, your contributions are celebrated, and your career can truly flourish.

GLOBAL OUTREACH

At MGI, we empower employees to broaden their horizons through international assignments and cross-border initiatives. We are committed to fostering global exposure, enabling our team to engage with diverse cultures, develop international expertise, and drive success worldwide.

Employee Assistance Program

Flexible working hour

Employees should be stress-free when reaching their workplace. MGI offers flexible working hours to provide employees a balance of work & life.

Pick & Drop Service

We provide a convenient employee transportation service, prioritizing female safety. Higher-level staff receive personal transport, reflecting our commitment to employee well-being and smooth commutes.

RECOGNIZING & REWARDING EXCELLENCE

At MGI, annual increments, performance evaluations, and promotions ensure employees are recognized and rewarded for their contributions. Our commitment to growth and advancement keeps our team motivated and valued. Join us and experience a workplace where your efforts are appreciated.

Job Rotation

We offer a well-planned career progression for our future business leaders to have an exposure of end to end business knowledge through consistent job rotation based on each employee’s competence.

On time Salary

We motivate our Employees by providing them salary on the first day of each month. This plays a major role to create impact in the life of our employees.

HEALTH & SAFETY

At MGI we believe in the old adage - Health is Wealth. We focus on employee health & wellbeing. Apart from on-site Medical Team, we have solutions for emergencies.

Provident Fund

We have recognized provident fund for the eligible employees to support financially incumbents whenever they need to plan for their future.

Retirement Benefits

Employees are provided retirement benefits after getting retired from his/her duty. We take good care of the security money of every employee and ensure that they get all the support.

EARNED LEAVE

We provide Earned Leave facility, which enables our employees to maintain their work-life balance. Earned Leave includes encashment and carry forward options.

Safety & Awareness

As an employer, MGI takes immense pride in proving one of the safest and healthiest workplace for its employees. Maintaining a safe and harmonious environment for the employees has been our top priority. Our administration team members are relentlessly working to improve safety measures for our factory workers as well as corporate offices. We provide all kinds of safety equipment and training to our employees to ensure their safety. Moreover, we have our own ambulances, firefighting teams, infirmary to ensure emergency response in fastest possible time.

Special Facilities

 
 

Food & Hygiene

A well furnished canteen facility is a supplementary system facilitated by MGI for employees. Many employees in our organization use canteen services as they do not have the means to prepare their own food or are unable to get it.

HRIS Software

We introduced one stop solution software for our employees which automated the whole process of our day to day work.

Prayer Room

A well arranged prayer room is here for employees who practice religion requiring prayer at specific times.


EOM

Purchasing MGI products in a discounted price is available for MGI employees through Employee Order Management (EOM). Head Office, Fresh House, Fresh Cottage and Ctg Office employees are eligible for this facility till now.
