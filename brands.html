Himadri Limited Limited:

Himadri Limited Overview

Himadri Limited is providing modern cold storage facilities to its customers based in 6 (six) different places of north Bengal, Bangladesh. The company plays a vital role in the agricultural supply chain of the whole country, primarily focusing on the storage of table potatoes and seed potatoes. North Bengal, known for its fertile lands and favorable climate, is a major hub for potato cultivation in Bangladesh. The cold storage locations allow it to serve the storage needs of both large companies and small-scale farmers, making it a crucial player in the local agricultural economy as well as ensure its contribution to the national economy.
